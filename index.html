How to connect

1

Make sure your phone and the Cast-compatible display (Chromecast, etc.) are on the same Wi-Fi network.

2

Tap "Cast to Car Display" and select your device from the list.

3

OpenRoad loads on the car screen. Your phone can go to sleep — the session keeps running.

4

Use the car's touchscreen to interact. Return here only to disconnect.
